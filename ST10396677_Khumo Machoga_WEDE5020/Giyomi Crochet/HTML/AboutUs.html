Creator & Owner of Giyomi Creations

I am a 20-year-old girl who has a passion for art.And what better way to show my creative side than through something that will last forever. I took an interest in crochet and knitting since I was 16 years-old.I wanted to create items that I would buy from a store.I started making my friends and myself pieces from little projects and made them in my spare time. I'm always trying to take inspiration from other creators projects from things like a pattern I've seen on a jersey to blankets. I have never seen creators create bags in South Africa from these designs so I decided to create bags from patterns I've seen. I hope you'll carry a little piece of mywild imagination to your next outing!
